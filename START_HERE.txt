CraftPath - PoE2 Crafting Optimizer
====================================

TWO WAYS TO USE IT:

1) ONLINE (no setup) - the free crafting optimizer
   If someone hosts CraftPath, just visit the URL. You get the full crafting
   path optimizer: cheapest route to your target mods, odds, costs, and
   desecration/putrefaction modeling. No login, no cookie, nothing to install.

2) DESKTOP (this download) - optimizer PLUS live market pricing
   Runs on your machine with your own PoE session, like Path of Building.
   Live price-check and profit scanning work here because your session cookie
   stays on your computer and never touches anyone's server.

   FIRST TIME SETUP:
   a) Install Python 3.12+ from python.org (check "Add to PATH" during install)
   b) Open this folder in a terminal and run:  pip install -r requirements.txt
   c) Get your session cookie: log in at pathofexile.com, press F12,
      Application > Cookies > pathofexile.com > copy the POESESSID value.
   d) Open RUN_DESKTOP.bat in Notepad, paste your cookie after POESESSID=,
      save, then double-click RUN_DESKTOP.bat.
   e) Open http://127.0.0.1:5000

   Your cookie is account-equivalent - never paste it into a website, chat, or
   share it. It only belongs in RUN_DESKTOP.bat on your own machine.

   To refresh currency prices anytime:  python prices.py
   (then re-run RUN_DESKTOP.bat)
